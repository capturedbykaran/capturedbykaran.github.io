More About Me

I’m Karan, an enthusiastic Engineer and Photographer passionate about transforming creativity into meaningful digital experiences. Currently pursuing a B.Tech in Instrumentation and Control Engineering at Netaji Subhas University of Technology (NSUT), I have built a strong foundation in C++, Python, and full-stack web development. My academic focus includes Data Structures, Algorithms, and Database Systems, which help me craft scalable, efficient, and reliable solutions.

Alongside academics, I actively work on personal and freelance projects involving web design, front-end development, and creative media production. I enjoy blending logic and aesthetics — whether it’s developing a responsive web app or composing a perfect shot behind the camera. My portfolio includes a range of projects demonstrating my ability to integrate technical precision with visual storytelling.

As a photographer and videographer, I find beauty in detail and emotion in motion. My work captures the connection between people, places, and perspectives, using tools like Adobe Photoshop, Lightroom, and Premiere Pro to turn raw visuals into compelling stories. This fusion of engineering and artistry defines my identity — a creator who builds as well as captures.

I’m constantly exploring new technologies, frameworks, and artistic styles to grow both as a developer and as a creative professional. My goal is to collaborate on impactful projects that challenge me to innovate and deliver meaningful results. Whether it’s coding a web solution or curating a photo series, I aim to blend precision, creativity, and passion in everything I do.
